## 📥 How to Download and Install?

Installation takes only a few seconds via your browser's **Developer Mode** (Chrome, Brave, Edge).

1.  **Download the files**:
    *   Go to **`releases`** button (Middle right), then Download the zip **`corresponding`** to your version .
    *   If you're on firefox you don't have to extract the folder so skip to step 2. If not keep following.
    *   Extract the folder to your desktop.
2.  **Open Extensions**:
    *   Go to your browser settings and look for "Extensions" (or type `chrome://extensions/` in your address bar).
3.  **Enable Developer Mode**:
    *   Toggle the **"Developer mode"** switch in the top right to **ON**.
4.  **Load the Bot**:
    *   Click on **"Load unpacked"**.
    *   Select the extracted folder (the one containing `manifest.json`).
5.  **Pin the extension**:
    *   Click the "Puzzle" icon 🧩 and pin **NEXTERA BOT** for quick access.

## 🚀 Required Action to Start

The bot needs to "sniff" the action to learn how to reproduce it. **Without this step, the widget will remain red.**

1.  **Open Discord** in your browser and go to the **Last Meadow Online** channel.
2.  **Launch the Activity**: Click the **green leaf** icon in the top right of Discord.
    *   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dd849599-a93e-491f-a813-a1b0920364db" width="300">
3.  **The "Mother" Action (Required)**: 
    *   Press to start the adventure.
    *   **Spam the "Adventure" button** until you fill the bar **once** completely.
    *   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7b19b192-03bd-42a0-aca1-6105f0861c06" width="300">
4.  **You're Ready!**: 
    *   Once the bar is filled for the first time, the widget will turn **GREEN** 🟢.
    *   The bot will take over from here.
    *   **Tip:** You can go do something else! Just **keep your browser open** (you don't necessarily need to stay on the Discord tab).

## 💡 Useful Info & Tips

*   **🕹️ Free Movement**: Click the blue `NEXTERA BOT` header to **drag the overlay** anywhere on your page.
*   **📉 Compact Mode**: Use the **[-]** button to shrink the widget. Click **[+]** to see your stats again.
*   **🌍 Multi-site support**: Via the popup, choose to display the widget everywhere or **Discord only**.
*   **🚫 Hide the Widget**: You can hide the overlay using the **"HIDE WIDGET"** button in the popup without stopping the bot.
*   **⚠️ Security**: If the widget turns **YELLOW**, the bot is taking a 5s break to avoid Discord's "Rate Limit."
